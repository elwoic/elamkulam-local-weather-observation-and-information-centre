Our Mission

ELWOIC is an independent, real-time weather monitoring project dedicated to serving accurate and reliable weather details for the Elamkulam community in Malappuram, Kerala. We provide timely, accessible data that empowers residents, farmers, and local institutions to make informed decisions about daily activities, agriculture, and safety.

🎯

Purpose & Value

The goal of ELWOIC is to serve the Elamkulam community with easy-to-understand, local weather information. Whether planning daily activities, travelling, or staying safe during sudden weather changes, users can rely on this platform for real-time local observations and forecasts tailored specifically to this region.

Important Information

Technical & Operational Details
  • Weather data is updated at least every 30 minutes using multiple API sources — AccuWeather, Windy, IMD Kerala, OpenWeatherMap, and Open-Meteo.
  • The station covers approximately a 15-kilometre radius around coordinates 10.892363, 76.234402 with continuous automated monitoring.
  • For technical issues with radar display, we automatically switch to Windy radar for uninterrupted monitoring.
  • Details shown on the site may vary slightly across sources. Contacting us is always a good option if you notice any discrepancy.
  • Air quality data is measured locally using an AirGradient monitoring device provided through the AirGradient community programme.
