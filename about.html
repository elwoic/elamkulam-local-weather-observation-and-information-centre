Our Mission and History

Who We Are

The Elamkulam Local Weather Observation & Information Centre (ELWOIC) was established with the core mission of providing hyper-local, accurate, and timely weather data to the residents and businesses of the Elamkulam region. Our dedication is to community safety and preparedness, turning complex meteorological data into understandable, actionable insights.

Our Core Values

  • Accuracy: Utilizing advanced local sensor networks for precise readings.
  • Community: Serving as a vital resource for disaster readiness and daily planning.
  • Transparency: Making all observational data easily accessible to the public.
  • Innovation: Constantly improving our forecasting models and presentation.

Meet the Team

Our team is comprised of passionate local volunteers, meteorology enthusiasts, and data specialists committed to ensuring the reliability of our services. We believe that informed communities are resilient communities.
