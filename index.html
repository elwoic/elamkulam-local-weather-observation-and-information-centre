About ELWOIC

The Elamkulam Local Weather Observation and Information Centre (ELWOIC) is a community-driven initiative dedicated to monitoring and sharing local weather data for Elamkulam, Kerala. Our goal is to provide accurate, timely, and locally relevant weather information for awareness and research.
