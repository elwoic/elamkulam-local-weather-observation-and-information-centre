About ELWOIC

ELWOIC (Elamkulam Local Weather Observation & Information Centre) is a student-run community project dedicated to providing near real-time local weather information for Elamkulam, Kerala.

Data Sources

Our data is aggregated from multiple sources (Open-Meteo, Weather widgets, Windy embeds and local sensors when available). This page documents the partners and sources used for forecasts and live snapshots.

Equipment & Methods

We use a combination of web APIs and community sensors. Data is refreshed periodically and displayed on the site. Measurements may vary from official government sources and are provided for local awareness only.

Disclaimer

ELWOIC is a student project and not an official meteorological authority. For official warnings and advisories, always refer to the India Meteorological Department (IMD) or local authorities.
